About Me

Hello, I'm Tekenatei Franklyn

An Associate Product Manager and an ALX-trained Front-End Software Engineer based in Nigeria. Technology has always been my passion, and I am dedicated to using it to solve real-world problems. I'm at the beginning of my professional journey, driven by a love for technology and a desire to create impactful solutions. My focus is on developing my skills as a product manager and front-end engineer, aiming to bring innovative products to life that improve people's lives. The journey has been incredibly rewarding, allowing me to blend my technical skills with my passion for product management. Looking ahead, I am eager to continue growing as a technologist and leader. My goal is to work on modern, innovative projects that push the boundaries of what's possible, especially in AI and blockchain. I also run a YouTube channel where I provide guidance and insights to young people, sharing my journey and lessons learned.
